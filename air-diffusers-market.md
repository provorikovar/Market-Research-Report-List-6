<p><p>Air diffusers are essential in providing proper air circulation and distribution in various applications. There are two main types of air diffusers in the market - Porous Plate Type and Porous Tube Type. </p><p>Porous Plate Type diffusers evenly disperse air through tiny pores on a flat plate, creating gentle airflow. Porous Tube Type diffusers allow for air distribution through a series of small holes along a tube, providing a more focused and directional airflow. Both types offer efficient air distribution solutions for different environments and applications.</p></p>
