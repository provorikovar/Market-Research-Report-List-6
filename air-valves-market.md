<p><p>Air valves are essential components in water distribution systems to maintain optimal system performance. There are three main types of air valves in the market - Air-Release Valves, Air/Vacuum Valves, and Combination Valves. Air-Release Valves are used to release air pockets from pipelines, Air/Vacuum Valves release both air and prevent vacuum formation, while Combination Valves combine both functions. Each type serves a specific purpose in maintaining system efficiency and preventing damage from air pockets and vacuum formation.</p></p>
