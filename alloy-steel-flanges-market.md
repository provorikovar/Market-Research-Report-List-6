<p><p>The Alloy Steel Flanges market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Blind flanges are used to seal piping ends, while weld neck flanges facilitate strong connections through welding. Slip-on flanges offer easy installation by sliding over the pipe, and socket weld flanges provide a secure joint by fitting into a socket. Other flange types may include threaded or safety flanges, each designed to meet diverse industrial requirements for durability, pressure resistance, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
