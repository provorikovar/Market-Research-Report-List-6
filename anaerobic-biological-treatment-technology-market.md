<p><p>Anaerobic Biological Treatment Technology encompasses two main market types: Batch Type and Continuous Type. Batch Type systems process wastewater in discrete batches, allowing flexibility in operation and effective treatment for varying influent characteristics. In contrast, Continuous Type systems maintain a steady flow of wastewater, promoting ongoing degradation of organic matter and resulting in more efficient energy production. Both types utilize anaerobic microorganisms to convert organic waste into biogas, offering sustainable solutions for waste management and energy recovery.</p></p>
