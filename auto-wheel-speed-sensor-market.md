<p><strong>The Auto Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto wheel speed sensor market is divided into two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors detect changes in the magnetic field produced by the wheel's rotation, providing precise speed measurements crucial for traction control and ABS systems. Magnetic Electric sensors, on the other hand, generate an electrical signal based on the rotation of a magnetic component, offering a reliable and cost-effective solution for detecting wheel speed. Both types play a vital role in modern automotive safety and performance.</p></p>
