<p><p>The automotive adhesive and sealants market primarily includes three types: epoxy, acrylic, and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives offer high strength and durability, making them ideal for bonding metal and composite materials. Acrylic adhesives provide rapid curing and excellent resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for exterior applications. Polyurethane sealants exhibit flexibility and adhesion to various substrates, often used for sealing joints and gaps. Each type serves distinct applications within automotive manufacturing, enhancing performance, longevity, and safety.</p></p>
