<p><p>Automotive exhaust heat recovery systems comprise several technologies aimed at improving efficiency. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) captures and utilizes waste heat from engine exhaust, enhancing overall energy use. Rankine Cycle Systems convert heat into mechanical energy using a working fluid. Thermoelectric Generators directly convert thermal energy into electricity through temperature differentials. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) integrates a generator with a turbocharger to reclaim energy, reducing engine load and boosting efficiency. Each system contributes to improved fuel economy and reduced emissions.</p></p>
