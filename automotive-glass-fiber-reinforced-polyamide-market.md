In contrast, short fiber reinforced polyamide incorporates chopped glass fibers, offering improved dimensional stability and processing flexibility, suitable for various automotive applications. Together, these types provide a range of solutions for manufacturers seeking lightweight, durable materials to enhance vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
