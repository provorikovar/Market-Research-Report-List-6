<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Hemming Adhesive Market is categorized into two main types: One Component and Two Component adhesives. One Component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, providing ease of application and consistent performance. They cure through exposure to environmental factors or heat. In contrast, Two Component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before application, offering superior bond strength and durability. Both types serve critical roles in automotive manufacturing, enhancing structural integrity and aesthetics in vehicle assembly.</p></p>
