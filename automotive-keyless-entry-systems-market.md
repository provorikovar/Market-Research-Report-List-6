<p><p>Automotive keyless entry systems enhance convenience and security by allowing vehicle access without traditional keys. The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) market utilizes radio waves to communicate between the vehicle and a key fob, enabling entry when in proximity. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology, on the other hand, enables secure interactions through low-energy wireless connections, allowing features like remote access and unlocking via smartphones. Both technologies aim to provide seamless entry experiences while improving vehicle security against unauthorized access.</p></p>
