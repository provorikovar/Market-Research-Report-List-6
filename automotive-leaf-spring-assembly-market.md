<p><p>The automotive leaf spring assembly market comprises two primary types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple stacked layers, providing enhanced strength and load-bearing capacity, making them suitable for heavier vehicles and applications requiring high durability. In contrast, mono-leaf springs feature a single, flexible layer that offers a lighter weight and simpler design, ideal for smaller vehicles and those seeking improved handling and ride comfort. Both types serve distinct needs in automotive suspension systems.</p></p>
