<p><strong>The Automotive LED Driver ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step-up Driver ICs</li><li>Step-down Driver ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive LED driver ICs are essential components for efficient lighting systems in vehicles, categorized mainly into step-up and step-down driver ICs. Step-up driver ICs, or boost converters, increase input voltage to power high-brightness LEDs, often used in headlights and fog lights. In contrast, step-down driver ICs, or buck converters, reduce voltage to drive LED lighting in dashboards and interior lamps. Both types ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency, enhancing safety and aesthetics in automotive applications.</p></p>
