<p><p>The automotive pedal sensor market is primarily divided into two types: brake pedal sensors and accelerator pedal sensors. Brake pedal sensors detect the position and force applied to the brake pedal, enhancing vehicle safety by managing braking systems and integrating with advanced driver assistance features. Conversely, accelerator pedal sensors monitor throttle input, enabling precise engine control for optimal performance and fuel efficiency. Both sensors are crucial for electronic throttle control systems, contributing to improved driving dynamics and overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
