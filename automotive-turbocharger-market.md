<p><ul><li>Mechanical Turbocharging</li><li>Exhaust Gas Turbocharging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Turbocharger market is divided into two main types: Mechanical Turbocharging and Exhaust Gas Turbocharging. Mechanical Turbocharging involves the use of a mechanical compressor to increase the pressure of the incoming air before it enters the engine, resulting in improved combustion and power output. On the other hand, Exhaust Gas Turbocharging utilizes the energy from the engine's exhaust gases to drive a turbine connected to a compressor, enhancing engine performance and efficiency. Both types of turbocharging play a significant role in improving the overall performance of vehicles.</p></p>
