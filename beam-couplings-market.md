<p><ul><li>Single Beam Couplings</li><li>Multi Beam Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam couplings market is primarily categorized into single beam couplings and multi beam couplings. Single beam couplings feature a single flexible beam, enabling precise alignment and accommodating misalignment, making them ideal for low-torque applications. In contrast, multi beam couplings consist of multiple beams, offering greater flexibility and torque transmission capabilities. These are suitable for high-torque applications and environments with significant misalignment. Both types play crucial roles in industries requiring efficient torque transfer and precise movement.</p></p>
