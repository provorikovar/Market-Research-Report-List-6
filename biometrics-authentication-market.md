<p><p>The biometrics authentication market includes various systems such as hand-based recognition, eye-based recognition, and vein pattern-based recognition. Hand-based systems analyze fingerprints or hand geometry for user identification. Eye-based systems, including iris and retina recognition, capture unique patterns in the eye for secure access. Vein pattern recognition utilizes infrared light to analyze the unique vascular patterns in an individual's fingers or palms. Each type offers varying levels of security and convenience, catering to different applications across industries.</p></p>
