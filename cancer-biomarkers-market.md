<p><p>The cancer biomarkers market is divided into two main types: protein biomarkers and genetic biomarkers. Protein biomarkers are specific proteins produced by cancer cells or in response to cancer, aiding in diagnosis and prognosis. Genetic biomarkers involve alterations in DNA or RNA that indicate a predisposition to cancer or its progression. Both types play crucial roles in personalized medicine, allowing for targeted therapies and better treatment outcomes by providing insights into the cancer’s characteristics and behavior.</p></p>
