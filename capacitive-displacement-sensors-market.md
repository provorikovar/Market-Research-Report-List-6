<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Capacitive Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Polar Distance Type</li><li>Variable Area Type</li><li>Variable Medium Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive displacement sensors are categorized into three main types based on their measurement principles. The Variable Polar Distance Type relies on the change in capacitance due to varying distances between the sensor and the target. The Variable Area Type measures changes in capacitance resulting from alterations in the overlapping area of two conductive plates. The Variable Medium Type detects changes in capacitance caused by the introduction of different dielectric materials between the sensor plates, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
