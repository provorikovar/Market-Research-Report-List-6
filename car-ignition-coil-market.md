<p><p>The car ignition coil market is segmented into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark coils generate one strong spark per ignition cycle, ideal for conventional engines, ensuring efficient fuel combustion and reliable starting. In contrast, multi-spark coils generate multiple sparks within a single cycle, enhancing fuel atomization and combustion efficiency, particularly in high-performance or modified engines. As automotive technology advances, the demand for these ignition coil types varies, influencing market dynamics and consumer preferences.</p></p>
