<p><p>Crack meters are specialized instruments used to monitor the movement and opening of cracks in structures. The market includes mechanical crack meters, which utilize physical gauges to measure displacement, providing reliable, straightforward readings. On the other hand, wire crack meters employ a wire or cable system to detect changes in crack width with high precision. Both types are critical in geotechnical and civil engineering applications, enabling professionals to assess structural integrity and prevent potential failures through ongoing monitoring.</p></p>
