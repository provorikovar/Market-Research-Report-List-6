<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CdZnTe detector market features several types designed for various applications. Pixelated detectors comprise an array of small pixels enhancing spatial resolution. Coplanar grid detectors utilize a flat electrode design for improved energy resolution. Planar-type detectors have a uniform surface and are effective for general detection. Quasi-hemispherical detectors feature a semi-spherical shape for enhanced sensitivity to radiation. Housed quasi-hemispherical detectors provide a protective casing, offering robustness while maintaining the performance advantages of their quasi-hemispherical counterparts.</p></p>
