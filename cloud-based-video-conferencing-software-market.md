<p><strong>The Cloud Based Video Conferencing Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC Architecture</li><li>AVC Architecture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloud-based video conferencing software market is primarily categorized into two main architectures: SVC (Scalable Video Coding) and AVC (Advanced Video Coding). SVC architecture allows for efficient scaling of video quality and bandwidth management, enabling a more flexible user experience, especially in varying network conditions. In contrast, AVC is a widely adopted standard that offers high-quality video compression but lacks the scalability feature, making it less adaptable to dynamic conditions. Both architectures serve distinct needs within the video conferencing ecosystem.</p></p>
