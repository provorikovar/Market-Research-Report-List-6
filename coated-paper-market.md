<p><strong>The Coated Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coated Chemical Pulp</li><li>Coated Mechanical Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coated paper market comprises primarily two types: coated chemical pulp and coated mechanical pulp. Coated chemical pulp is produced using chemical processes that enhance brightness and strength, making it ideal for high-quality printing applications. In contrast, coated mechanical pulp is derived from mechanical processes, resulting in lower brightness but offering economic advantages. Both types are utilized in various industries, including publishing and packaging, with coated chemical pulp typically favored for premium products and coated mechanical pulp for cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
