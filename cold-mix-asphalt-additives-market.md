<p><strong>The Cold Mix Asphalt Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li><li>Solvent-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Mix Asphalt Additives market is primarily divided into two types: emulsion-based and solvent-based additives. Emulsion-based cold mix asphalt additives utilize water and bitumen emulsions to enhance adhesion and stability, making them suitable for various weather conditions and easier to apply. Solvent-based cold mix asphalt additives, on the other hand, use organic solvents to improve the workability and performance of the asphalt mixture, offering flexibility in application but often requiring more careful handling due to volatile components.</p></p>
