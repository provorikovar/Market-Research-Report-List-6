<p><p>The combustion thermal spray gun market features several key types: Flame Spraying utilizes a fuel-oxygen mixture to melt and project material onto surfaces, producing coatings with strong adhesion. HVOC Spraying, or High Velocity Oxy-Fuel, operates at higher velocities, creating denser, finer coatings for enhanced performance. Detonation Flame Spraying employs controlled detonations to achieve extremely high temperatures, resulting in unique coating properties and improved bonding. Together, these methods cater to diverse applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
