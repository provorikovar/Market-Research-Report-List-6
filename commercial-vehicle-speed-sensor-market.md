<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle speed sensor market primarily features two types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, offering accurate and reliable measurements. They are commonly used in modern vehicles due to their durability and quick response time. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors rely on magnetic fields generated by a rotating shaft to produce electrical signals, making them suitable for various applications. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle safety and performance monitoring.</p></p>
