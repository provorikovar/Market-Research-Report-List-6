<p><p>Composite insulated panels are categorized into several types based on insulation materials. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) panels are lightweight, affordable, and offer good thermal efficiency. PUR/PIR (Polyurethane/Polyisocyanurate) panels provide higher thermal insulation and are suitable for various climates. Mineral wool panels are fire-resistant and ideal for acoustic insulation. Additionally, glass wool panels offer excellent insulation with reduced weight, making them suitable for warehouse applications. Other panels include advanced materials catering to specific performance needs and applications in construction.</p></p>
