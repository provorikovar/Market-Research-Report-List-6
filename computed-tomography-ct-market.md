<p><p>Computed Tomography (CT) is a medical imaging technique that combines X-ray measurements taken from various angles and uses computer processing to create cross-sectional images of bones, organs, and tissues. This growth is driven by technological advancements such as the development of portable and high-resolution CT scanners, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and an increasing emphasis on early disease detection. Furthermore, the integration of artificial intelligence in CT imaging enhances diagnostic accuracy and workflow efficiency.</p><p>Market trends indicate a shift towards hybrid imaging systems that enhance diagnostic capabilities by combining CT with other modalities such as MRI and PET. Additionally, the demand for CT imaging is growing in emerging markets due to improvements in healthcare infrastructure and increased awareness of diagnostic imaging benefits. Continued innovation and increasing investment in healthcare infrastructure are further anticipated to fuel market growth.</p></p>
