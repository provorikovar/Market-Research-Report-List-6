<p><p>The contact and convective dryers market is categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and operational principles. Horizontal dryers facilitate efficient heat transfer across the surface, making them suitable for various materials, while vertical dryers utilize a gravitational flow, ideal for bulk material processing. Both designs optimize drying efficiency, with horizontal models often preferred for controlled environments, while vertical systems offer space-saving advantages and are commonly used in industries like food processing and agriculture.</p></p>
