<p><strong>The Contactless Smart Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>RFIC</li><li>RFCPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contactless smart card market comprises three main types: RFID (Radio Frequency Identification), RFIC (Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit), and RFCPU (Radio Frequency Central Processing Unit) cards. RFID cards utilize passive technology, relying on an external reader for power and communication. RFIC cards include integrated circuits that enable data processing and storage, often used in access control. RFCPU cards incorporate a microprocessor, allowing for more complex operations, security features, and application versatility, making them suitable for electronic payments and secure identification.</p></p>
