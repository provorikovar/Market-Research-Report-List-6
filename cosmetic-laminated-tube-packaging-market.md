<p><strong>The Cosmetic Laminated Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic laminated tube packaging primarily includes two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes consist of layers of aluminum and plastic, providing excellent protection against light, moisture, and oxygen, making them ideal for products like creams and ointments. In contrast, PBL tubes are made entirely of plastic materials, offering flexibility and lightweight properties, which appeal to manufacturers aiming for cost-effective solutions. Both types cater to diverse cosmetic product needs while ensuring product integrity and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
