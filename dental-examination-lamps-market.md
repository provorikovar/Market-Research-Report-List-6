<p><p>The dental examination lamps market features primarily two types: LED and halogen lamps. LED lamps are increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and low heat emission, making them ideal for extended use in dental practices. They provide bright, focused light that enhances visibility during procedures. In contrast, halogen lamps offer high-intensity illumination and a consistent color temperature, but they consume more energy and generate more heat. Both types cater to different practitioner preferences and operational needs in dental settings.</p></p>
