<p><strong>The Direct-write Electron Beam Lithography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gaussian beam EBL Systems</li><li>Shaped beam EBL Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-write electron beam lithography (EBL) systems are essential for high-resolution microfabrication. Gaussian beam EBL systems use a focused, symmetrical electron beam for precision patterning, ideal for applications requiring fine details. In contrast, shaped beam EBL systems manipulate the beam shape, allowing for rapid patterning with larger areas, improving throughput. Both types cater to distinct market needs: Gaussian systems excel in resolution-critical tasks, while shaped beam systems are preferred for efficiency in larger-scale designs and quicker turnaround times.</p></p>
