<p><p>The disposable dental articulators market comprises three main types: flat type, slot type, and oblique type. Flat type articulators offer a simple, straightforward design for basic occlusal assessments. Slot type articulators provide adjustable features for more precise positioning of dental casts, enhancing accuracy in alignments. Oblique type articulators are designed for complex dental procedures, allowing for multi-directional adjustments that mimic natural jaw movement. Each type serves distinct functions, catering to varying clinical requirements in dental practices.</p></p>
