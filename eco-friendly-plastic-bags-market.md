<p><p>The eco-friendly plastic bags market encompasses three main types: Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn starch and is compostable, making it popular for single-use applications. PHA is produced by microbial fermentation of organic materials, offering excellent biodegradability and versatility. Starch Blends combine starch with polymers, creating biodegradable options suitable for various applications. Together, these materials provide sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics, addressing environmental concerns and promoting circular economy practices.</p></p>
