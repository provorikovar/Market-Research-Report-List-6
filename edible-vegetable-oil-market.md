Olive oil, known for its flavor and health benefits, is a staple in Mediterranean cuisine. Cottonseed oil is commonly used in processed foods. Corn oil, derived from corn, is often used in baking. Coconut oil, popular for its unique taste, is known for its saturated fat content. Nut oils, like walnut or almond, add specific flavors to dishes, while other oils cater to niche markets.</p></p>
