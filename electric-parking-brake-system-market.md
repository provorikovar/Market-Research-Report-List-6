<p><ul><li>Caliper Integrated EPB</li><li>Cable Puller EPB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Parking Brake (EPB) system market is primarily categorized into two types: Caliper Integrated EPB and Cable Puller EPB. The Caliper Integrated EPB incorporates the braking mechanism within the wheel's caliper, offering compact design and reduced weight, enhancing vehicle efficiency. In contrast, the Cable Puller EPB utilizes a traditional cable system to engage the parking brake, providing a cost-effective solution. Both types aim to improve vehicle safety and convenience while enabling advanced features like auto-release and integration with vehicle systems.</p></p>
