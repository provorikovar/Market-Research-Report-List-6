<p><ul><li>External Heated Soldering Iron</li><li>Internal Heated Soldering Iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External heated soldering iron is a type of electric soldering iron where the heating element is separate from the soldering tip, allowing for faster heating and cooling times. This type is commonly used for heavy-duty soldering jobs in industries. On the other hand, internal heated soldering iron has the heating element built into the soldering tip, providing more precise control over temperature and better heat retention. This type is often preferred for delicate electronic soldering tasks in industries such as electronics and telecommunication.</p></p>
