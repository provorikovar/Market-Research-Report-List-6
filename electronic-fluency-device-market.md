<p><ul><li>In-the-ear</li><li>Behind the Ear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Fluency Device market offers two primary types: In-the-ear (ITE) devices and Behind-the-ear (BTE) devices. ITE devices are custom-fitted to the user's ear canal, providing a discreet option for those seeking speech support. In contrast, BTE devices sit behind the ear, with a tube delivering sound into the ear. BTE devices typically offer more power and battery life, making them suitable for users with various levels of speech fluency challenges, enhancing communication abilities effectively.</p></p>
