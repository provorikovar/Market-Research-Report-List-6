<p><p>Electroplating chemicals are substances used in the electroplating process to coat a conductive surface with a metal layer. Additionally, rising focus on corrosion resistance and surface finish enhancement in various applications is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Key trends in the electroplating chemicals market include the shift towards trivalent chromium plating as a more environmentally friendly alternative to hexavalent chromium, growing demand for nickel-free plating solutions due to health concerns, and increasing adoption of automation and robotics in plating processes for improved efficiency and productivity.</p></p>

<p><p>Electroplating chemicals market can be classified into three types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents are used to prepare the surface of the substrate before electroplating, ensuring good adhesion and uniform coating. Electroplating additives help in improving the quality, efficiency, and appearance of the plated surface. Post-treatment agents are used to enhance the durability, corrosion resistance, and overall performance of the plated surface after the electroplating process.</p></p>
