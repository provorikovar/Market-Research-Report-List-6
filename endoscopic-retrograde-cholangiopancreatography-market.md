<p><p>The Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) market includes two primary types: fiber duodenoscopes and electronic duodenoscopes. Fiber duodenoscopes utilize flexible fiber optics to transmit light and capture images, offering a more affordable option but with limitations in image quality. In contrast, electronic duodenoscopes incorporate advanced imaging technology, providing enhanced visualization and diagnostic capabilities. Both types serve critical roles in bile duct and pancreatic duct analysis, with electronic duodenoscopes often preferred for their superior performance in complex procedures.</p></p>
