<p><p>The epoxy-polyurethane adhesive market features several types catering to diverse applications. Foaming adhesives expand on application, filling gaps and providing insulation. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, perfect for metal bonding. Conductive adhesives allow electricity to flow between components, essential in electronics. Cryogenic adhesives maintain strong bonds at extremely low temperatures, suitable for aerospace and cryogenic applications. Other types include specialized formulations like heat-resistant or water-resistant adhesives, addressing varied industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
