<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent weather resistance and flexibility. The market for EPDM is primarily segmented into two types based on the polymerization method: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving the monomers in a solvent, leading to high-quality rubber with specific properties. In contrast, suspension polymerization disperses the monomers in water, resulting in a different molecular structure. Each method affects the final product's characteristics and application suitability.</p></p>
