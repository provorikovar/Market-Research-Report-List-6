<p><p>The fire retardant heat shrink tubing market consists of two main types: single wall and dual wall tubing. Single wall tubing is designed for general insulation and protection, shrinking to conform to irregular shapes and providing fire-resistant properties. In contrast, dual wall tubing features an inner adhesive layer that provides additional moisture and environmental protection, making it ideal for harsh conditions. Both types enhance safety and durability in electrical applications, ensuring reliable performance under extreme temperatures and potential fire hazards.</p></p>
