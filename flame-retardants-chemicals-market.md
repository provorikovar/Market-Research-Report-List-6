<p><p>The flame retardants chemicals market is divided into two main types: non-halogenated and halogenated flame retardants. Non-halogenated flame retardants are compounds that do not contain halogen elements, often offering reduced toxicity and environmental impact. They are commonly used in electronics, textiles, and building materials. Halogenated flame retardants, containing bromine or chlorine, are highly effective at inhibiting ignition but can pose health and environmental risks. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety across various industries.</p></p>
