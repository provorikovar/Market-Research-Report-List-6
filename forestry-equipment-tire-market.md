<p><p>The forestry equipment tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering flexibility and shock absorption, ideal for uneven terrain. Solid tires are made from rubber and provide durability and puncture resistance, making them suitable for harsh environments. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering resilience and reduced weight, while also being resistant to wear. Each type is designed to meet specific operational demands in forestry applications, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
