<p><p>Frequency converters are devices that convert the frequency of an electrical signal. There are two main types of frequency converters: static frequency converters and rotary frequency converters. Static frequency converters use electronic components to directly convert the frequency of an incoming signal. On the other hand, rotary frequency converters use a rotating machine to convert the frequency. Both types of frequency converters have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as aerospace, marine, and power generation.</p></p>
