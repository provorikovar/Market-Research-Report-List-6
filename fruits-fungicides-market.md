<p><p>The fruits fungicides market is divided into two main types: chemical synthesis fungicides and natural fungicides. Chemical synthesis fungicides are man-made compounds designed to effectively combat a wide range of fungal pathogens that affect fruit crops. They often have quick action and long-lasting effects but can pose environmental and health risks. These tend to be less potent but are favored for organic farming practices.</p></p>
