<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized steel bar market includes two main types: conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar and continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar. Conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar involves dipping steel bars in molten zinc, providing a robust corrosion-resistant layer. This method is widely used in construction for its durability. Continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar, on the other hand, employs a continuous production process, ensuring uniform coating and enhanced efficiency. Both types cater to the demand for long-lasting reinforcement in various construction applications.</p></p>
