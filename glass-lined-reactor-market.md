<p><p>The Glass Lined Reactor market can be categorized into three main types: AE Type, BE Type, and CE Type. AE Type reactors have an enamel coating only on the reactor body, while BE Type reactors have enamel coating on both the body and the agitator. CE Type reactors have an enamel coating on the external surface and a chemical resistance lining on the internal surface. Each type offers specific advantages based on the level of protection required for different chemical processes and industry applications.</p></p>
