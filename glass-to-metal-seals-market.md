<p><strong>The Glass-to-metal Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass-to-metal seals are used in various industries to create a tight and secure bond between glass and metal surfaces. Matched seals are designed to have thermal expansion properties that closely match the materials being sealed, reducing the risk of cracking or breaking due to temperature changes. Compression seals involve compressing the glass and metal components together to create a reliable seal. Both types of seals are important in applications where a strong, hermetic seal is required to prevent leakage or contamination.</p></p>
