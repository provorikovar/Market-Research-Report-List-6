<p><ul><li>Glaucoma Drainage Devices</li><li>Glaucoma Laser Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma surgical devices market primarily includes two types: glaucoma drainage devices and glaucoma laser systems. Glaucoma drainage devices are implants that help regulate intraocular pressure by facilitating the drainage of aqueous humor, thus preventing damage to the optic nerve. Glaucoma laser systems utilize advanced laser technology to create openings in the eye’s drainage system or reduce fluid production, providing a minimally invasive approach for pressure control. Both types are essential for managing glaucoma and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
