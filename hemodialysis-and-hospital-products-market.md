<p><p>Hemodialysis is a medical procedure that filters and purifies the blood using a machine, typically performed on patients with kidney failure. This treatment involves diverting blood from the body to an artificial kidney (dialyzer) where waste products and excess fluids are removed before the blood is returned to the body. Hospital products related to hemodialysis include dialyzers, dialysis machines, blood tubing sets, and other consumables essential for the effective delivery of dialysis therapy.</p><p>The Hemodialysis and Hospital Products Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.40% during the forecast period. Enhanced healthcare infrastructure and rising awareness about renal diseases further contribute to market expansion. Additionally, trends such as home dialysis and patient-centric care models are reshaping the landscape, leading to innovative product developments. This dynamic environment suggests a robust future for the hemodialysis and hospital products sector.</p></p>
