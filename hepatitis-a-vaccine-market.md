<p><strong>The Hepatitis A Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactivated Vaccine</li><li>Live Attenuated Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hepatitis A vaccine market consists of two main types of vaccines: inactivated vaccines and live attenuated vaccines. Inactivated vaccines contain killed virus particles that stimulate the immune system to produce antibodies against Hepatitis A without causing the disease. Live attenuated vaccines, on the other hand, contain weakened forms of the virus that can replicate in the body, leading to a stronger and longer-lasting immune response. Both types of vaccines are effective in preventing Hepatitis A infection and are widely used for immunization purposes.</p></p>
