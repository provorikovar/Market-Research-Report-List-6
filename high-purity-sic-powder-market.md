<p><p>The high purity silicon carbide (SiC) powder market is primarily divided into two main types: β-SiC and α-SiC. β-SiC is characterized by its cubic crystal structure, offering superior thermal conductivity and high strength, making it ideal for electronic applications and high-temperature environments. α-SiC, with its hexagonal structure, exhibits enhanced hardness and wear resistance, typically used in abrasive materials and refractory applications. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace, driven by their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
