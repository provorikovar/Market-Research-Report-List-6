<p><p>The household kitchen blenders market primarily comprises two main types: countertop blenders and immersion blenders. Countertop blenders are versatile appliances featuring a fixed jug and powerful motors, ideal for smoothies, soups, and sauces. They generally offer multiple settings and larger capacities. Immersion blenders, also known as handheld blenders, are compact devices that blend directly in pots or bowls, making them convenient for quick tasks and easy to clean. Both types cater to varying culinary needs and preferences in home kitchens.</p></p>
