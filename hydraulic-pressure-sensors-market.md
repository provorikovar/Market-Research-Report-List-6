<p><strong>The Hydraulic Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Type</li><li>Relative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic pressure sensor market is mainly categorized into differential and relative types. Differential pressure sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, making them ideal for monitoring systems where pressure gradients are crucial, like in filtration systems. Relative pressure sensors, on the other hand, measure pressure in relation to a reference pressure, typically atmospheric pressure. These sensors are widely used in applications where gauging pressure changes against the ambient environment is essential, such as in hydraulic systems and industrial machinery.</p></p>
