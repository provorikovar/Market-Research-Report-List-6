<p><ul><li>Low Pressure</li><li>Medium Pressure</li><li>High Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline pressure filter market is categorized into three types based on operating pressure: low, medium, and high pressure. Low-pressure filters typically handle up to 10 bar and are used in less demanding applications, ensuring basic particulate filtration. Medium-pressure filters operate between 10 to 25 bar, suitable for industrial operations requiring efficient separation of contaminants. High-pressure filters exceed 25 bar, designed for challenging environments like hydraulic systems and gas compression, offering robust filtration for critical applications.</p></p>
