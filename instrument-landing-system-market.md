<p><p>The Instrument Landing System (ILS) market is categorized into three main types: CAT I, CAT II, and CAT III based on their capabilities. CAT I ILS helps pilots during approach and landing in normal visibility conditions. CAT II ILS allows for lower visibility landing with higher precision than CAT I. CAT III ILS provides the highest level of precision and allows for landing in extremely low visibility conditions, making it the most advanced and reliable system for pilots during approach and landing.</p></p>
