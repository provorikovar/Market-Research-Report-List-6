<p><p>Instrumentation fittings in the tube and pipe market refer to components used to connect tubes or pipes in various industrial applications. Tube fittings are designed for use with tubing, while pipe fittings are specifically for use with pipes. Tube fittings are typically smaller in size and are commonly used in high-pressure applications, while pipe fittings are used for larger diameter pipes. Both types of fittings provide a secure and leak-proof connection, allowing for the efficient and safe operation of fluid systems in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation.</p></p>
