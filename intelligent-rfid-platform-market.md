<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF) RFID</li><li>High-Frequency (HF) RFID</li><li>Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent RFID Platform market is segmented into three key types: Low Frequency (LF) RFID, High-Frequency (HF) RFID, and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID. LF RFID operates at 125-134 kHz, offering short-range communication primarily used for animal tracking and access control. HF RFID, functioning at 13.56 MHz, is suitable for applications like library management and ticketing, benefiting from moderate range and data transfer rates. UHF RFID, operating between 300 MHz and 960 MHz, offers long-range capabilities ideal for inventory management, logistics, and supply chain tracking.</p></p>
