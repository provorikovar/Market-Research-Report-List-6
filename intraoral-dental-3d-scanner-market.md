<p><p>An intraoral dental 3D scanner is a specialized device that allows dentists to capture precise digital impressions of a patient's oral cavity. This technology improves the process of creating dental restorations, aligners, and other dental appliances by offering faster, more accurate, and more comfortable patient experiences compared to traditional impression methods. The scanners utilize advanced optical technologies and can generate high-resolution 3D models that can be easily integrated into CAD/CAM systems for improved treatment planning and execution.</p><p>The intraoral dental 3D scanner market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for minimally invasive dental procedures, advancements in digital dentistry, and rising patient expectations for comfort and convenience. Adoption of these scanners is further propelled by their ability to reduce chair time, enhance workflow efficiency, and improve the accuracy of dental work. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period, highlighting the expanding role of digital technologies in dentistry.
