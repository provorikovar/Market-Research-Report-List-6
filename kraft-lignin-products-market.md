<p><p>Kraft Lignin products are divided into two main market types: Softwood Kraft Lignin and Hardwood Kraft Lignin. Softwood Kraft Lignin is derived from softwood species such as pine and spruce, and is known for its high purity and light color, making it ideal for applications in industries like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. On the other hand, Hardwood Kraft Lignin is derived from hardwood species like birch and oak, and is often used in applications like adhesives and concrete additives due to its higher levels of impurities and darker color.</p></p>
