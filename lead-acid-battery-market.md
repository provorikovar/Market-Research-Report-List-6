<p><p>The lead-acid battery market primarily features two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and require no maintenance, providing safety and compactness, making them ideal for applications like UPS systems and solar energy storage. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, have a liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance. They are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications due to their cost-effectiveness and robustness. Each type caters to specific needs based on operational requirements.</p></p>
