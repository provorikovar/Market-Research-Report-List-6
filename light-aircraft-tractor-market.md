<p><strong>The Light Aircraft Tractor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Towbarless</li><li>With Towbar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light aircraft tractor market consists of two main types: towbarless and with towbar. Towbarless tractors are designed to attach directly to the aircraft's nose gear, allowing for greater maneuverability and reducing the risk of damage during towing. In contrast, tractors with towbars utilize a detachable hitch to connect with the airplane, providing stability and control, especially for larger aircraft. Both options are essential for efficient ground handling, catering to different operational needs in aviation infrastructure.</p></p>
