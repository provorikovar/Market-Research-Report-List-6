<p><p>The light industrial conveyor belts market can be categorized into two primary types based on production processes: coating and calendering. The coating process involves applying a layer of material, enhancing the belt's durability and resistance to various elements. This technique is often used to improve grip and reduce wear. Conversely, the calendering process involves compressing and heating materials to create a smooth, uniform surface, which is ideal for specific applications requiring consistent thickness and flexibility. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
