<p><p>The Lightweight Insulating Castable market consists of two primary types: Casting Type and Dry Gunning Type. Casting Type involves pouring a liquid mix into molds, allowing for detailed shapes while providing excellent thermal insulation. Dry Gunning Type, on the other hand, uses a dry mix that is pneumatically conveyed and shot onto surfaces, offering quick application and efficient coverage. Both types are essential for high-temperature industrial applications, enhancing energy efficiency and contributing to reduced operational costs.</p></p>
