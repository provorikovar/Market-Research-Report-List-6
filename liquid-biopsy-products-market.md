<p><strong>The Liquid Biopsy Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CTCs</li><li>ctDNA</li><li>Exosomes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid biopsy products market comprises three main types: circulating tumor cells (CTCs), circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA), and exosomes. CTCs are intact cancer cells shed into the bloodstream, providing insights into tumor characteristics. ctDNA consists of small fragments of DNA released by tumors, enabling the detection of genetic mutations. Exosomes are nanosized vesicles that carry proteins and RNA, facilitating intercellular communication and biomarker discovery. Together, these products enhance cancer diagnosis, treatment monitoring, and personalized medicine strategies.</p></p>
