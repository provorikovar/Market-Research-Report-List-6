<p><p>Magnetic powder cores consist of different types such as MPP, Sendust, High Flux, Fe-Si, and others. MPP cores are known for high saturation flux density, low core loss, and good temperature stability. Sendust cores offer high frequency performance and excellent DC bias characteristics. High Flux cores provide high induction levels and low losses. Fe-Si cores are popular for their low cost and good magnetic properties. Other types of powder cores include iron alloy and ferrite cores, each offering unique advantages in various applications.</p></p>
