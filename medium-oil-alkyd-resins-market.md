<p><ul><li>Dry Alkyd Resins</li><li>Non-Dry Alkyd Resins</li><li>Half Dry Alkyd Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium oil alkyd resins are categorized into three types based on drying characteristics. Dry alkyd resins cure rapidly, primarily through oxidation, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Non-dry alkyd resins remain tacky, often used in formulations requiring extended workability. Half dry alkyd resins offer a balance, drying moderately fast while maintaining some flexibility, suitable for diverse applications like coatings and inks. Each type caters to specific industry needs, influencing adhesion, durability, and finish quality in various products.</p></p>
