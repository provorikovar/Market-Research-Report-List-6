<p><p>MEMS-based resonators are categorized into series resonance and parallel resonance types. Series resonance types exhibit a high frequency response, which is ideal for applications requiring precision timing and low power consumption, commonly used in communication devices. In contrast, parallel resonance types feature a higher quality factor and are suited for applications needing improved signal selectivity and stability, such as sensors and oscillators. Both types drive innovation in various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications.</p></p>
