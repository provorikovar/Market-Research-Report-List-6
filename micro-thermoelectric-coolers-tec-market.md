<p><p>The Micro Thermoelectric Coolers (TEC) market is segmented into two main types: Single Stage and Multistage Micro Thermoelectric Coolers. Single Stage TECs are designed for applications requiring modest temperature differentials, ideal for compact cooling solutions. In contrast, Multistage TECs utilize multiple thermoelectric junctions, allowing for higher temperature differentials and enhanced cooling performance, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Both types are essential in industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices, where precise temperature control is critical.</p></p>
