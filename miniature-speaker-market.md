<p><p>The miniature speaker market includes single-magnet and dual-magnetic speakers, each catering to distinct audio needs. Single-magnet speakers utilize one magnet for a compact design, often preferred for space-constrained applications like portable devices. This differentiation allows manufacturers to target specific segments, balancing size, sound quality, and cost-effectiveness in a rapidly evolving audio landscape.</p></p>
