<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Manual</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile surgery table market comprises four main types: electric, manual, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Electric tables offer effortless height and position adjustments with powered controls, enhancing precision during surgeries. Manual tables require physical effort for adjustments, making them less convenient. Hydraulic tables utilize fluid power for height changes, providing smoother movements but may be less precise. Electro-hydraulic tables combine electric controls and hydraulic systems, offering optimal versatility and stability, making them suitable for various surgical procedures and environments.</p></p>
