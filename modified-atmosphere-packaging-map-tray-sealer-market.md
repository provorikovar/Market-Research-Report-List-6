<p><p>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) Tray Sealer is a technology that enhances the shelf life of perishable products by modifying the atmospheric composition within a sealed packaging. This method typically involves reducing oxygen levels and increasing inert gases like nitrogen or carbon dioxide, effectively slowing down spoilage and preserving freshness. MAP tray sealers are vital in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and electronics, where product integrity is crucial.</p><p>The Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) Tray Sealer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer demand for fresh and minimally processed foods.
