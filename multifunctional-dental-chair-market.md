<p><ul><li>Air Control Dental Chair</li><li>Electronic Control Dental Chair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional dental chair market comprises two primary types: air control dental chairs and electronic control dental chairs. Air control dental chairs utilize pneumatic systems to operate movements, offering smooth adjustments with minimal mechanical complexity, making them reliable and easy to maintain. In contrast, electronic control dental chairs employ electric motors for precise and automated positioning, providing enhanced comfort and functionality, often integrating advanced features such as memory presets and digital controls, catering to modern dental practice needs.</p></p>
