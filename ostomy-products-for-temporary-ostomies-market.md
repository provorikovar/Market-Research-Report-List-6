<p><p>Ostomy products for temporary ostomies include one-piece and two-piece bag systems. One-piece bags combine the skin barrier and pouch into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use, ideal for patients seeking convenience. In contrast, two-piece systems separate the pouch from the skin barrier, allowing for easier pouch changes and better skin care management. Both types cater to different patient needs, ensuring comfort and functionality during the transitional period of recovery from surgery or injury.</p></p>
