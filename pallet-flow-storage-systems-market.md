<p><p>Pallet Flow Storage Systems are a dynamic storage solution designed to enhance warehouse efficiency by utilizing gravity to move pallets through a structured rack system. These systems consist of inclined lanes with rollers that allow pallets to flow automatically from the loading side to the picking side. This FIFO (First-In, First-Out) method optimizes space utilization and ensures easy access to inventory, making it ideal for high-turnover goods.</p><p>The Pallet Flow Storage Systems Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for efficient warehousing and distribution solutions across various sectors, including FMCG, pharmaceuticals, and e-commerce.
