<p><ul><li>Compressor Pasteurizer</li><li>Water Circulation Pasteurizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pasta pasteurizers market consists of different types, primarily compressor pasteurizers and water circulation pasteurizers. Compressor pasteurizers utilize refrigeration technology to quickly cool and heat the pasta, ensuring efficient pasteurization while maintaining texture and flavor. In contrast, water circulation pasteurizers use heated water to circulate around the pasta, providing a uniform temperature for effective pasteurization. Both types are essential in the food processing industry to enhance safety, extend shelf life, and preserve the quality of pasta products.</p></p>
