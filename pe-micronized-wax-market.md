<p><p>The PE Micronized Wax market comprises two main types: Polyethylene Waxes and Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes. Polyethylene Waxes are low molecular weight polymers used for their excellent lubricating properties and durability in various applications, including coatings and plastics. Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes, on the other hand, are produced through the oxidation of polyethylene and are characterized by their increased polarity and compatibility with polar substrates, making them ideal for applications such as adhesives, sealants, and formulations requiring enhanced adhesion and stability.</p></p>
