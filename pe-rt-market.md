<p><p>PE-RT, or Polyethylene of Raised Temperature Resistance, is a type of plastic material used for pipes. There are three main market types of PE-RT based on the comonomer used during production - Ethylene-Octene, Ethylene-Hexene, and Ethylene-Butene. These market types differ in terms of their mechanical properties, temperature resistance, and flexibility. Ethylene-Octene PE-RT offers high flexibility and impact strength, Ethylene-Hexene PE-RT provides improved temperature resistance, and Ethylene-Butene PE-RT is known for its high creep resistance and stress crack resistance.</p></p>
