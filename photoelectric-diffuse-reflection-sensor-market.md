<p><p>The photoelectric diffuse reflection sensor market is segmented into PNP and NPN types. PNP sensors, when activated, supply positive voltage to the output, making them suitable for applications where a positive signal is needed. Conversely, NPN sensors provide a negative or ground signal when triggered, often preferred in systems using negative logic. Both types utilize light to detect objects and are widely used in automation, packaging, and production lines, with specific choices depending on the electrical compatibility of the system.</p></p>
