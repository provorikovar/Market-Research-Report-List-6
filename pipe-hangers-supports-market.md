Pipe supports, on the other hand, provide stability at designated points along the pipeline, preventing movement due to vibrations or thermal expansion. These components are crucial for maintaining system integrity, minimizing wear, and facilitating maintenance, contributing to the overall efficiency and safety of fluid transport systems.</p></p>
