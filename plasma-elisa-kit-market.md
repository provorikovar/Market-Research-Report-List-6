<p><p>Plasma ELISA Kits are used for detecting and quantifying specific proteins in blood plasma. The Sandwich method involves capturing the target protein between two antibodies, providing highly sensitive results. The Direct method measures the target protein directly using a single antibody, suitable for smaller molecules. Other methods include Competitive ELISA and Indirect ELISA, offering alternate ways to detect proteins in plasma. Each method caters to different protein detection needs, providing flexibility and options for researchers and clinicians in the plasma ELISA kit market.</p></p>
