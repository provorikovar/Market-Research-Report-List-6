<p><strong>The Plastic Clad Silica Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV/VIS</li><li>VIS/NIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic Clad Silica Fiber (PCSF) is categorized into two main types based on their wavelength transmission capabilities: UV/VIS and VIS/NIR. UV/VIS fibers are designed for ultraviolet to visible light applications, ideal for sensing and imaging in scientific and medical fields. In contrast, VIS/NIR fibers operate in the visible to near-infrared range, making them suitable for telecommunications, spectroscopy, and industrial uses. Both types leverage the flexibility of plastic cladding while benefiting from the low-loss characteristics of silica fibers.</p></p>
