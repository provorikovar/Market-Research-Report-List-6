<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plenoptic cameras are divided into two main types: Standard plenoptic cameras and Focused plenoptic cameras. Standard plenoptic cameras capture all of the light rays in a scene, allowing for post-capture refocusing and depth information. Focused plenoptic cameras, on the other hand, have a fixed focus lens that captures more detailed depth information at the time of capture. Both types of cameras have their advantages and are used in various applications such as photography, computer vision, and virtual reality.</p></p>
