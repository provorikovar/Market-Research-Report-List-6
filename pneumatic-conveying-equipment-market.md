<p><p>Pneumatic Conveying Equipment market can be segmented into three main types based on the pressure system used: Positive Pressure Conveying, Vacuum Pressure Conveying, and Combined Conveying. Positive Pressure Conveying utilizes high pressure to push materials through the system, while Vacuum Pressure Conveying uses suction to pull materials. Combined Conveying combines both positive pressure and vacuum pressure systems for maximum efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
