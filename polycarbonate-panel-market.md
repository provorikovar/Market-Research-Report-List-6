<p><p>The polycarbonate panel market encompasses three main types: multi-wall panels, corrugated panels, and solid panels. Multi-wall panels consist of multiple layers, offering excellent insulation and light diffusion, making them ideal for energy-efficient applications. Corrugated panels feature a wavy design that enhances strength and rigidity, suitable for roofing and cladding. Solid panels are thick and durable, providing high impact resistance and transparency, commonly used in glazing and safety applications. Each type caters to specific needs in construction and design.</p></p>
