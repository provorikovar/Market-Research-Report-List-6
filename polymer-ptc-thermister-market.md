<p><p>The Polymer PTC Thermistor market comprises two main types: Surface Mount Type and Radial Leaded Type. Surface Mount Thermistors are compact and designed for efficient placement on circuit boards, ideal for high-density applications. In contrast, Radial Leaded Thermistors feature leads extending from each side, suitable for through-hole mounting, offering more robust physical connections in various electronic applications. Both types provide overcurrent protection and are widely used in automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment for temperature sensing and regulation.</p></p>
