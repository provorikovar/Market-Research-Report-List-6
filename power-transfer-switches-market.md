<p><p>The Power Transfer Switch (PTS) market consists of two primary types: automatic and manual switches. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) seamlessly switch the electrical load from the main power source to a backup generator during an outage, ensuring uninterrupted power supply. In contrast, manual transfer switches require manual intervention to switch power sources, offering simpler and often more cost-effective solutions for smaller applications. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing power reliability and operational efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
