<p><p>Power transmission and distribution conductors are essential components in the electrical grid, facilitating the transfer of electricity from generation sites to consumers. The market mainly features types like Aluminum Conductors (AAC) and Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced (ACSR). AAC conductors are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, making them ideal for short spans. ACSR conductors combine aluminum’s conductivity with steel's strength, providing higher tension capabilities, making them suitable for longer transmission distances. Both types play crucial roles in efficient power delivery.</p></p>
