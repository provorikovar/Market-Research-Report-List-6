<p><p>Prosthetic liners are flexible, cushioned interfaces that are used to enhance the comfort and functionality of prosthetic limbs. These liners serve to provide a barrier between the residual limb and the prosthetic socket, helping to reduce friction, improve suspension, and offer shock absorption. The increasing prevalence of amputations due to diabetes, vascular diseases, and accidents is driving the demand for advanced prosthetic solutions.</p><p>Market growth for prosthetic liners is expected to be significant, driven by technological advancements and a focus on improving user comfort and mobility. Key trends include the integration of smart materials and 3D printing technology, which allow for customized and lightweight liners that better conform to individual limb shapes. Additionally, there is a growing emphasis on developing liners that offer antimicrobial properties, enhancing hygiene and reducing the risk of infections. The rise in awareness regarding rehabilitation and the availability of advanced healthcare services are further contributing to the growth of this market, ultimately aiming to improve the quality of life for amputees.</p></p>
