<p><p>The PTFE membrane market is classified into hydrophobic and hydrophilic types, along with others. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water while allowing gases and vapors to pass, making them ideal for filtration and breathable applications. Hydrophilic PTFE membranes, on the other hand, attract water and are used for applications requiring moisture management, such as in medical and biological fields. Additional types may include specialized membranes designed for niche markets or unique functional requirements, broadening their application range across various industries.</p></p>
