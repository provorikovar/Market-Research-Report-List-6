<p><strong>The PVD Equipment for Solar Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Evaporation</li><li>Sputter Deposition</li><li>Arc Vapor Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment in the solar cell market includes several techniques. Thermal evaporation involves heating materials to create vapor, allowing them to condense on substrates, forming thin films. Sputter deposition uses energetic particles to dislodge atoms from a target, which then deposit onto the solar cell surface. Arc vapor deposition generates a plasma arc to vaporize materials, leading to a high-quality film formation. Each method offers unique benefits in efficiency and film uniformity, impacting solar cell performance.</p></p>
