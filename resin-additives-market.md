<p><p>The resin additives market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions to enhance resin properties. Fillers improve mechanical strength and reduce costs, while colorants provide aesthetic appeal. Plasticizers enhance flexibility and workability, making materials more versatile. Antioxidants protect resins from degradation due to heat and oxidation, ensuring longevity. Stabilizers prevent the deterioration of resins during processing and in end-use applications, maintaining performance. Other additives may include agents for specific properties, such as flame retardants or UV stabilizers, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
