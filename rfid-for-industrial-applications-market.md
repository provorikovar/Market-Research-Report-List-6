<p><strong>The RFID for Industrial Applications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF)</li><li>High Frequency (HF)</li><li>Ultra High Frequency (UHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology is pivotal in industrial applications, categorized into three main frequency types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF RFID operates between 125-134 kHz, allowing for short-range communication, often used for animal tracking and access control. HF RFID, operating at 13.56 MHz, is commonly employed for item tracking and ticketing. UHF RFID, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, enables longer read distances and faster data transfer, ideal for inventory management and supply chain optimization.</p></p>
