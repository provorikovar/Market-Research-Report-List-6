<p><strong>The Rigid Overhead Catenary System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Catenary</li><li>Stitched Catenary</li><li>Compound Catenary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rigid Overhead Catenary System market is categorized into three types: Simple Catenary, Stitched Catenary, and Compound Catenary. Simple Catenary features a single curve line and straightforward design, ideal for low-speed applications. Stitched Catenary combines multiple segments for improved stability and flexibility, suitable for varying terrain. Compound Catenary merges both linear and curved sections, optimizing height and clearance while accommodating high-speed operations. Each type is tailored for specific operational needs, enhancing efficiency in rail and transit systems.</p></p>
