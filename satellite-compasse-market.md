<p><p>The satellite compass market encompasses two main types: GPS compasses and GNSS compasses. GPS compasses utilize the Global Positioning System to determine location and navigation, primarily relying on signals from satellites for positioning accuracy. In contrast, GNSS compasses incorporate multiple satellite systems, including GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, allowing for enhanced accuracy and reliability in various environments. Both types serve critical roles in navigation across diverse applications, such as marine, aviation, and land-based operations.</p></p>
