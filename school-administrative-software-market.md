<p><p>The School Administrative Software market can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based school administrative software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure and allows for automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises school administrative software is installed locally on school servers, providing greater control and security, but requiring more maintenance, higher initial costs, and limited access.</p></p>
