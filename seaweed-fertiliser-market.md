<p><p>The seaweed fertilizer market encompasses various production methods, each impacting nutrient availability and efficiency. Chemical hydrolysis utilizes chemical agents to break down seaweed, enhancing nutrient release. Physical extraction involves mechanical processes to obtain essential compounds while preserving integrity. The enzymolysis process employs specific enzymes to catalyze the breakdown of seaweed, optimizing nutrient extraction. Lastly, biological fermentation leverages microbial activity to decompose seaweed, yielding a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer. Each method offers unique advantages for sustainable agriculture.</p></p>
