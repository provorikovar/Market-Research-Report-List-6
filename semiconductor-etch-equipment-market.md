<p><ul><li>Dry Etch Equipment</li><li>Wet Etch Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etch equipment market is categorized into two main types: dry etch equipment and wet etch equipment. Dry etch equipment utilizes plasma or gases to remove material from the semiconductor wafer surface, offering high precision and control, making it suitable for advanced manufacturing processes. In contrast, wet etch equipment employs liquid chemicals to dissolve unwanted materials, providing a simpler and cost-effective solution for less intricate tasks. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor fabrication process, enabling the production of integrated circuits.</p></p>
