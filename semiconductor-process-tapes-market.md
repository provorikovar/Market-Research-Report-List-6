<p><p>The semiconductor process tapes market is divided into two main types: UV tape and non-UV tape. UV tape is designed to adhere during processing and can be easily removed using ultraviolet light, making it ideal for applications where residue-free removal is critical. Non-UV tape, on the other hand, offers strong adhesion and durability without requiring UV exposure for removal, catering to various applications in packaging and bonding. Both types serve vital roles in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
