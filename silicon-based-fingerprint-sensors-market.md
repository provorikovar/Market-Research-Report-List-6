<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensors market features two primary types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors capture fingerprints by sliding a finger across the sensor's surface, making them portable and cost-effective for mobile devices. In contrast, area sensors capture the entire fingerprint image at once, providing higher accuracy and detail, making them suitable for secure applications in devices like laptops and high-security systems. Each type serves distinct user needs, impacting market dynamics and technological advancements.</p></p>
