<p><p>The single wafer cleaning tools market is categorized into two main types: dry process cleaning tools and wet process cleaning tools. Dry process cleaning tools utilize techniques like plasma or ion beam cleaning to remove contaminants without using liquids, making them suitable for delicate substrates. In contrast, wet process cleaning tools employ chemical solutions and rinse steps to effectively eliminate particles and impurities from wafers. Both methods are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring high purity and performance of wafers.</p></p>
