<p><p>Skin grafting system market offers various types of dermatomes including knife dermatomes, drum dermatomes, electrical dermatomes, and air-powered dermatomes. Knife dermatomes are manual instruments used for harvesting skin grafts. Drum dermatomes use a rotating drum to gather skin grafts. Electrical dermatomes use electric power for precise and consistent skin harvesting. Air-powered dermatomes operate with compressed air for efficient skin graft collection. These different types cater to different needs and preferences of healthcare professionals performing skin grafting procedures.</p></p>
