<p><p>The Sodium Hyaluronate market is primarily divided into two types: microbial fermentation and animal tissue extraction. On the other hand, animal tissue extraction involves obtaining sodium hyaluronate from animal sources, typically from rooster combs or other tissues, which may raise concerns about ethical sourcing and variability in product quality. Both types cater to diverse applications in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and medical devices.</p></p>
