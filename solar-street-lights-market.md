<p><strong>The Solar Street Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar street lights market is divided into two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, equipped with solar panels, batteries, and LED lights, making them ideal for remote areas without electricity. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights are integrated into the local power grid, allowing excess energy generated to be fed back into the grid. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing energy efficiency with accessibility and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
