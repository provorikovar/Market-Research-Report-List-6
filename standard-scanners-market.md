<p><strong>The Standard Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flatbed scanners</li><li>Feed-through scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The standard scanner market is primarily segmented into flatbed and feed-through scanners. Flatbed scanners feature a stationary glass surface where documents are placed, allowing for high-quality, detailed scanning of various media, including books and photos. In contrast, feed-through scanners utilize a mechanism to automatically transport documents through the scanner, enhancing speed and efficiency, particularly for high-volume scanning tasks. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to different user requirements in both personal and professional settings.</p></p>
