<p><p>The Synthetic Cordierite market is segmented into two main types: Non-Porous Cordierite and Porous Cordierite. Non-Porous Cordierite is characterized by its dense structure, offering high thermal stability and resistance to thermal shock, making it ideal for applications like kiln furniture and ceramic substrates. In contrast, Porous Cordierite features a more permeable structure that allows for better airflow, making it suitable for applications like catalyst supports and filtration systems. Both types serve distinct industrial needs based on their physical properties.</p></p>
