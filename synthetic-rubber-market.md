<p><p>The synthetic rubber market encompasses several key types: Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR) is widely used in tires and footwear; Polybutadiene Rubber (BR) offers superior abrasion resistance, making it ideal for tires and industrial applications. Styrenic Block Copolymer (SBC) is valued for its flexibility and durability. Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) is resistant to heat and ozone, suitable for roofing and automotive seals. Butyl Rubber (IIR) is known for its low permeability, while Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) excels in oil resistance. Other types include various specialty rubbers with diverse applications.</p></p>
