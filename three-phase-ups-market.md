<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Three-phase UPS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offline/standby Three-phase UPS</li><li>Line-interactive Three-phase UPS</li><li>Online/double-conversion Three-phase UPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase UPS market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Offline/Standby Three-phase UPS**: It provides basic backup power, activating during outages and relying on the utility supply during normal operation.</p><p>2. **Line-Interactive Three-phase UPS**: This type offers enhanced performance, regulating voltage fluctuations while maintaining efficiency through its inverter during outages, without a complete switchover.</p><p>3. **Online/Double-Conversion Three-phase UPS**: It delivers continuous power and voltage regulation by converting incoming AC to DC and back to AC, ensuring maximum protection and power quality at all times.</p></p>
