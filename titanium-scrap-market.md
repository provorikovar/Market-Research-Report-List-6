<p><p>The Titanium Scrap market can be categorized into various types based on the form of titanium waste. Mixed Titanium Solids refer to a combination of different types of titanium waste. Titanium Turnings are small pieces of titanium metal remaining after manufacturing processes. Titanium Swarf consists of fine metal shavings or filings. RUTILE Scraps are waste materials from the production of rutile, a form of titanium dioxide. Titanium Sponge / Residues are remnants of sponge titanium production. Other types of titanium scrap include miscellaneous waste materials related to titanium processing.</p></p>
