<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave ToF</li><li>Gate ToF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) Ranging Sensor market features two primary types: Continuous Wave ToF and Gate ToF. Continuous Wave ToF sensors operate by emitting a consistent light wave, measuring the phase shift of the reflected signal to determine distance. In contrast, Gate ToF sensors utilize time-gated pulses, capturing light reflections at specific intervals, enabling high-resolution depth sensing. Both types are crucial in applications like autonomous vehicles, robotics, and consumer electronics, enhancing spatial awareness and environmental interaction capabilities.</p></p>
