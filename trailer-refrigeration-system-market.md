<p><strong>The Trailer Refrigeration System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Temperature</li><li>Multi-Temperature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trailer refrigeration system market is categorized into single temperature and multi-temperature types. Single temperature systems maintain a consistent temperature throughout the trailer, ideal for transporting homogenous cargo like frozen foods. In contrast, multi-temperature systems feature separate compartments that allow for varying temperatures within the same trailer, enabling the transport of diverse products, such as perishable fruits and frozen goods simultaneously. This flexibility enhances operational efficiency, meeting the demands of a broader range of temperature-sensitive goods.</p></p>
