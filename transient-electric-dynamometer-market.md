<p><ul><li>Air Cooled</li><li>Water Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transient Electric Dynamometer market is categorized into two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled dynamometers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat generated during testing, making them efficient for lower power applications and easier to maintain. In contrast, water-cooled dynamometers use a liquid cooling system to manage higher heat loads, making them suitable for high-performance environments. Each type serves specific applications, balancing factors like cooling efficiency, installation space, and maintenance requirements to meet diverse industry needs.</p></p>
