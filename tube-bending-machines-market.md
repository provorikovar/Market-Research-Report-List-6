<p><p>Tube bending machines are classified into three main types based on their power source: Fully Electric Tube Bending Machines, Hybrid Tube Bending Machines, and Hydraulic Tube Bending Machines. Fully Electric machines are powered solely by electricity, offering precise control and energy efficiency. Hybrid machines combine electric and hydraulic power for enhanced flexibility and speed. Hydraulic machines are powered by hydraulic systems, providing high force capabilities for bending thick-walled tubes. Each type caters to different needs and requirements within the tube bending industry.</p></p>
