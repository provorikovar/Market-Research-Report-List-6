<p><p>Tumor fluid biopsy market includes three main types of detection techniques - circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) detection, circulating tumor cell (CTC) detection, and other market segments. ctDNA detection involves analyzing fragments of tumor DNA circulating in the blood, providing insights into genetic mutations and tumor heterogeneity. CTC detection focuses on capturing and analyzing tumor cells that have broken away from the primary tumor and entered the bloodstream. Other market segments may include exosome analysis, proteomic analysis, or multi-omics approaches for comprehensive tumor profiling.</p></p>
