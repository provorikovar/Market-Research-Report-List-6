<p><p>The veterinary amino acids feed additives market is categorized into essential and non-essential amino acids. Essential amino acids are those that animals cannot synthesize and must be obtained through their diet, playing a critical role in growth, reproduction, and overall health. Non-essential amino acids, on the other hand, can be produced by the animals themselves, but adding them to feed can enhance nutritional balance and promote better health outcomes. Both types are vital for optimizing animal nutrition and performance.</p></p>
