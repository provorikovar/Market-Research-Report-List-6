Permanent magnet systems use permanent magnets to produce vibration, offering a cost-effective solution for certain testing requirements. Both systems are used for durability testing, performance evaluation, and quality control in industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and military. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring product reliability and safety.</p></p>
