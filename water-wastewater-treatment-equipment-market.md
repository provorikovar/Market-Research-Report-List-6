<p><p>The Water and Wastewater Treatment Equipment market comprises various technologies, including media filtration and membrane filtration equipment. Media filtration involves using granular materials like sand or gravel to remove suspended solids and impurities from water. In contrast, membrane filtration utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate contaminants from water, effectively removing bacteria, viruses, and larger particles. Both types are essential for ensuring clean, safe water supply and efficient wastewater treatment, catering to industrial, municipal, and residential needs.</p></p>
