<p><ul><li>High Vacuum Drainage</li><li>Low Vacuum Drainage</li><li>Gravity Drainage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wound drainage sets are essential in managing post-surgical fluid accumulation. High vacuum drainage utilizes negative pressure to efficiently remove excess fluids, ensuring rapid healing. Low vacuum drainage applies gentle suction, allowing for controlled fluid removal, ideal for less invasive procedures. Gravity drainage relies on the natural force of gravity to transport fluids away from the wound site, typically used in simpler cases. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, enhancing patient recovery and minimizing complications.</p></p>
