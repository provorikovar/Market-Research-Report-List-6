<p><strong>The X-Ray Radiation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors</li><li>Charge Coupled Device Detectors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Radiation Detector market is categorized into several types. Flat Panel Detectors utilize a flat surface to capture images, offering high image quality and fast processing. Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors use photostimulable phosphor plates to capture X-ray images and convert them into digital format. Charge Coupled Device (CCD) Detectors rely on an array of light-sensitive elements to convert X-ray photons into electronic signals, ensuring high resolution. Other market types may include film-based systems and portable detectors, catering to varied imaging needs.</p></p>
